Salami Sandwich

Description

Delicious sandwich, with all aspects of diet considered. Only for your enjoyment.

Ingredients

  • Two pieces of bread
  • A few pieces of salami
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Keep both pieces of bread facing up
  2. Put some barbecue sauce on each side facing up
  3. Put some lettuce on both sides (not too much though)
  4. Place the salami on top of everything
  5. Join the pieces of bread
  6. Eat.
